PDF Margin Cropper

Trim white space or margins from every page of a PDF. Adjust each side independently with a live preview, then download the cropped file. Everything runs locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

-Pages
-Page size (pt)

Margins are cropped from each side as a percentage of the page size, so it works for pages of any size. The same crop is applied to all pages. This is a fast, lossless crop — page content outside the new boundary is simply hidden, not re-rendered.
